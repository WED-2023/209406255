Q&A

q1. 3 main diffs between web1, web2 & web3:

  • web1 (static): Websites are read-only with no interaction, just like an online newspaper.
  • web2 (social): Users can create and share content (social media, blogs, comments).
  • web3 (decentralized web): Uses blockchain and smart contracts for privacy, security, and decentralized control.

q2. example use case -> searching for "side effects of drugs (medications)":

  • Web1: A simple static webpage with a list of side effects, no user interaction.
  • Web2: A forum or social media where users share experiences and discuss medication side effects.
  • Web3: A decentralized medical database where doctors and patients securely share verified information using blockchain.
